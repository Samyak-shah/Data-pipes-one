Is road safety linked to alcohol consumption and does it reflect in car insurance prices by state? Here each blue dot is a different state.

This graph was made to see if there is any trend in the density of bars by people and the number of fatal car accidents for every state. I would have expected either a positive or negative relationship. I got neither. My original idea ws to map bars on google maps (bar density by geograpgy) and get data about local car accidents and see if there is any pattern. But that kind of data was difficult to find so I had to drop the idea and settle for more course grained analysis.

This graph was to see if general trends in road safety by state afects the car insurance or not.
